HOW TO SIGN UP FOR A PANCAKESWAP ACCOUNT?

Follow the below-mentioned steps to set up a wallet on Pancake Swap Login:

  • Firstly, open the MetaMask Wallet.
  • From there, click on the Network Selector bar appearing on your screen.
  • Now, scroll down and click the “Custom RPC” button.
  • After that, you have to provide the below-given information:
    • New RPC URL 
    • Chain ID 
    • Network Name 
    • Currency Symbol 
    • Block Explorer URL
  • After correctly filling in all the details, click on the Save button.
  • Then, move to the PancakeSwap Login extension or visit https://pancakeswap.finance/.
  • From the Homepage that appears on the screen, click the Connect bar located in the top-right corner of your screen.
  • Lastly, a pop-up window will appear on your screen where you need to select the Wallet you would like to connect to.
  • Now, select the Metamask option.

That’s it! You are now done with the steps to sign up for a PancakeSwap account.

HOW DOES PANCAKESWAP WORK?

Well, if you are new to PancakeSwap exchange, it is important to know how it exactly works. The pancake swap app or webpage actually works with BEP-20 tokens. In the Automated Market Maker (AMM) model, traders and investors trade against a liquidity pool comprising funds pooled in by multiple users altogether. Every time when you deposit your supported crypto tokens into liquidity pools, you will get liquidity provider (LP) tokens in exchange. These tokens actually represent your share in the particular liquidity pool.

You can trade on the pancake swap exchange platform only against liquidity pools. LP tokens are generated mainly when you add tokens in pairs, like BUSD-BNB, USDT-BUSD, etc. You can add liquidity or trade CAKE tokens to PancakeSwap and earn rewards, in the form of LP.
